Thor's Thunderbread

A dense, golden bread charged with the power of Mjölnir ⚡.
Served during feasts of thunder and storms, this bread is said
to give stamina to warriors and a booming voice to poets. Crisp
on the outside, soft like lightning clouds on the inside.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups ground wheat from the fields of Vanaheim
  • 1 cup buttermilk from the sacred goats of Tanngrisnir
  • 2 tbsp wild honey from yggdrasil'shighest branch
  • 1 tsp coarse salt, struck by lightning
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbps baking soada
  • 2 tbsp melted butter from the fires of Musepelheim

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200*C
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, and baking soda.
  3. Add buttermilk and honey, stir until dough forms.
  4. Knead briefly with strom-forged hands, but do not overwork.
  5. Shape into a round loaf and score a lightning bolt on the top
    with a sacred blade.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden and booming with crust.
  7. Brush with Muspelheimbutter while hot. Serve with mead,
    stew, or tales of conquest
